About Us

Our Story

Estd On 4 June 2008, Kavitashree Homes started with an aim to give a comfortable and convenient lifestyle to every stratum of the society. It was founded by Mr. Jai Prakash Sinha and Mrs. Kavita Kumari. After 8 years of past experience in the real estate business, they established their own company.

The idea was to introduce the much-needed concept of a secure, self-contained gated community that gives everyone a personal paradise and allows them to leave the chaos of the claustrophobic city life and live a life that is calm, serene and inspiring in every way.

Today Kavitashree Homes has built more than 20 landmark residential Societies independently and nearly 50 in association with others. More than 500 buyers have chosen this as a trusted company and around 2000 families reside in the buildings constructed by Kavitashree Homes.

We are proud to be known for our uncompromising quality of work and moral values and ethics. We hope to maintain our unaltered dedication towards our work and put the best work forward.

Our Vision


Our vision is to provide a regal standard of living to common people who wants to build their dream homes and workspace. We have flourished a niche in Real Estate of Bihar that has an unwavering commitment towards developing the urban landscape that suits individual needs, budgets and likings.

Our vision is to also participate in the development of our homeland Bihar and generate employment for the people of our community.
